# Inbox Agent Guide

`vault/Inbox/` is for unmanaged intake.

Use it for:

- Lists of links to ingest.
- Brief human notes about why a source may matter.
- Reading queues.

Do not write final reports or deep notes here. Once a source becomes managed, PaC metadata must live in `library/`, reports in `vault/Reports/`, and deep notes in `vault/Notes/`.
